Star Citizen

Star Citizen is a living sci-fi universe where you fly ships, run missions, trade, mine, salvage, and explore—solo or with a crew. Every session can turn into a rescue, a dogfight, a heist, or an “oops” that becomes a story.

Current state (Alpha 4.6): Lifeline for Levski throws the ’verse into a system-wide emergency centered on Levski’s Molina Mold outbreak. Players are pulled into interconnected hauling, courier, escort, recovery, defense, and resource gathering missions across Nyx and Stanton, turning logistics and cooperation into front-line gameplay. Quality-of-life improvements arrive with Kel-To ship supply kiosks in major Stanton cities for fast access to engineering tools and essentials, alongside early cockpit Light Amplification Modules (LAMP) on select ships to improve low-light visibility and navigation.

Your gameplay loop can be peaceful or chaotic: haul cargo for profit, mine for resources, salvage wrecks, run support rescues, or chase high-risk PvP. The strength of the current alpha is how these systems collide—your “simple delivery” might be the difference between a station’s survival or collapse.
