Game Loops in Star Citizen

Star Citizen is built around interconnected gameplay loops that let players choose how they live in the ’verse. Whether you prefer calculated risk or chaotic encounters, each loop supports long-term progression, reputation, and emergent storytelling. No two sessions ever play out the same.

Cargo hauling and trading focus on moving goods across star systems, balancing profit against piracy, market shifts, and travel risk. Mining loops revolve around scanning, extracting, and refining valuable resources, while salvaging allows players to reclaim materials from wrecked ships and abandoned hulls.

Combat-oriented loops introduce high-stakes PvP and bounty-driven encounters, where law, reputation, and consequences matter. Exploration invites players to discover locations, anomalies, and stories hidden across planets and space. Medical and rescue gameplay ties everything together, responding to distress calls and turning failures into recovery missions instead of hard stops.
